\begin{abstract}
This is a collection of talks from the summer school ``Counting arithmetic 
objects'' at the Centre de Recherches Math\'ematiques, organized by Henri 
Darmon and Andrew Granville. Talks were given by a number of speakers. The 
theme was Bhargava and Bhargava-Shankar's recent work counting various types 
of arithmetic objects.  
\end{abstract}
